Our Features

Explore the different functionalities that we provide to make Earth a better place!

Educational Content

Access articles, videos, and glossaries to learn about environmental issues and solutions. Our educational content provides resources that empower you to take action and make a difference.

Advocacy

Get involved! Submit your ideas and help promote sustainability through advocacy. Together, we can raise awareness, drive change, and create a greener future for everyone.

Tools

Calculate your carbon footprint, energy consumption, and water usage to live more sustainably. Our tools help you understand your impact on the environment and guide you toward a more eco-friendly lifestyle.

Community Forum

Join discussions with like-minded individuals, share ideas, and advocate for environmental change. The forum is a place to connect and collaborate with others who are passionate about the planet.

Quizzes

Test your knowledge on environmental issues with fun and interactive quizzes! Challenge yourself and learn more about how you can contribute to solving climate change.

AQI & Temperature Reporting

Post and view AQI and temperature data to track air quality in your area. Stay informed about the state of the environment in your city and contribute to building a global network of real-time data.
